Our Mission

Founded in 1965 as an educational institution based in london, The Art & History Museum is committed to producing distinctive exhibitions and public programming events.

The Institute of Art & History museum collects, preserves, and interprets works of art of the highest quality, representing the world’s diverse artistic traditions, for the inspiration and education of the public and in accordance with our profession’s highest ethical standards and practices.
